GSTUDY    Northern Ontario Clerkship
COMMENT   The Northern Ontario Medical School sends its 63 Clinical Clerks to peripheral rural clinics
COMMENT   all over the area. Students rotate through different compulsory and elective placements.
COMMENT   The rotations are generally oriented more towards primary care. At each rotation they
COMMENT   are rated by two physicians and a nurse independently in the same four areas of competence
COMMENT   on a six point scale. Over the year they rotate typically through six to eight rotations.
